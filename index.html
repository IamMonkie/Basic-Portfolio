About Me

Greetings, My name is Todd Williams. I am originally from Louisiana and have lived in Raleigh about 2 years now. I have a bachelors degree in Digital Art with a concentration in 3D. I am, however, currently working as an electrical drafter/ designer for an engineering company in RTP. My hobbies include 3D modeling, tinkering with electronics and tech, and rock climbing.
